IPAC
Innovation Expert
2018 till now Lisbon
IPAC is the official Portuguese entity responsible for the accreditation of national certification bodies. Certifications such as the ISO9001 or NP4457, can only be emitted by accredited certification bodies which grants them international recognition. My role in IPAC is to evaluate certification auditors and processes for the innovation management systems national standards - NP4457. As I shadow the auditors in their clients organisation, I am exposed to a wide range of organisations and their strategies which naturally gives me a solid understanding of which approaches and methods provide the best results in terms of innovation performance.
