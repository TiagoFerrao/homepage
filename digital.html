This section shows the most relevant projects and products of what I call my "digital career". I started by creating (designing features and functions) technical software for the energy sector and slowly building up knowledge and tools, from UI/UX, basic html and lately some backend.

Now I am playing with design and frontend was you can see by this site, moving up the ladder of the fullstack development until I am able to develop and integrate my own digital tools on my innovation consulting services.

Sportspots
Sports Events Manager
2018 till now Lisbon
SportSpots is a digital platform to manage sport activities. I built this using methods and tools developed during my reseach thesis in digital platform development. I am currently looking for investment to bring it to the market. If you like bugs, you can peek the prototype is hosted here (only available for mobile). visit prototype
ENERGYOFF
Energy Management Software
2014 till 2019 Lisbon
EnergyOFF was an original design developed to support energy managers of non industrial facilities. It logs energy consumptions, provides integrated reports and has a little of AI that suggest some effeciency measures. video site
BIOATLAS
Biomass SIG Platform
2013 till 2017 Lisbon
Bioatlas was the first portuguese SIG for biomass resources in Portugal. I led this project while Energy Programme Manager INTELI together with Aveiro University, AREANATEJO, ICNF and CBE. The app is no longer online and its development was quite bumpy, but as my first digital project it represents one of the most relevant milestones of my digital career.
