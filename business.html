My role in IPAC is to evaluate certification auditors and processes for the innovation management systems national standards - NP4457. As I shadow the auditors in their clients organisation, I am exposed to a wide range of organisations and their strategies which naturally gives me a solid understanding of which approaches and methods provide the best results in terms of innovation performance.
