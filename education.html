Every now and then I need to spend a period learning something new. I tried to acquire basic knowledge in 3 areas I consider fundamental: Engineering (how things work), Economics (how the world works) and Managment (how corporations work). Nowadays I am focusing more on digital skills, namely data analysis and design. Data because that's my most recurring need and tool, and design because I was never able to drawn a single sketch but my heart craves for a creative outlet and I need tools other than my clumsy hands to express my ideas.
